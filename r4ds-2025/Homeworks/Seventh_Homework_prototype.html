Author

Mykola Utkin

Introduction

This analysis explores the spatial distribution of working conditions across Ukrainian oblasts using official labor statistics from 2015-2019. We examine the share of employees working in harmful conditions and visualize regional disparities through choropleth maps.

Research Objectives:

  1. Map the percentage of workers in harmful working conditions by oblast
  2. Identify regional patterns and disparities in workplace safety
  3. Analyze temporal changes between 2015 and 2019
  4. Visualize industrial concentration and its relationship to working conditions
